# Housing Market Data Storytelling Report

## Objective
Use statistical analysis and hypothesis testing to validate market trends and present actionable business insights.

## Key Findings
- Furnished houses generally command higher prices
- Larger areas strongly correlate with higher pricing
- Bedroom count impacts market valuation
- Strategic segmentation improves market understanding

## Statistical Validation
- Conducted T-test between furnished and unfurnished housing prices
- Validated significance of furnishing on pricing

## Business Recommendations
- Invest in furnished housing markets
- Focus on high-area premium segments
- Use segmentation for targeted pricing strategies

## Final Outcome
Data-backed strategic recommendations for housing market decision-making.
